Announcing ADCx Copenhagen 2026...

We’re thrilled to share that ADCx Copenhagen 2026 will take place at Aalborg University campus in Copenhagen on April 28, 2026.

ADCx is a community-driven event - created by engineers and researchers across the Danish audio and software space, in collaboration with the international Audio Developer Conference network. The aim is simple: a focused, technical meeting point for everyone working with digital audio technology across applications - from music technology to acoustics, speech and hearing.

The program will center on all aspects of software for audio:

  • signal processing and algorithms
  • machine learning and AI for audio
  • human–computer interaction
  • embedded systems and firmware
  • audio application and plugin development
  • frameworks, tools, and methodologies

Expect contributions from industry engineers, researchers, independent developers, students, and experts from the wider audio development, ADC and Danish Sound Cluster communities.

More details, including the Call for Talks and ticket launch, will follow soon. If you want updates, please follow ADC and sign up for the newsletter.

Save the date - April 28, 2026!

We look forward to gathering the community in Copenhagen.
